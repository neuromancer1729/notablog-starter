Learnings from 2019

Posted on Mon, Dec 30, 2019 journal

2019 was a year of bold decisions and a sudden plunge into entrepreneurship.

Take it slow

Not to rush into decisions,

It is okay to not have an opinion on all things,

It is okay to say, I don't know

Find your music

Optimize for time spent in state of flow,

it is important to find the tune to resonate with in daily activities,

creation environment (people, place, thoughts) to increate time spent in flow.

Thought Exercise: How to measure time spent in flow ?

Doing good at scale

I elaborate on this deeply in my post on Crony Capitalism [WIP]

Watching Brian Chesky speak about this during the YC dinner was one of the highlights of 2019. Separate blog post on that piece coming soon.

Limits of rationality

Rationality isn't fit to make all decisions of life

just like money isn't the solution to solve all your problems

but just like how money will solve your money problems,

rationality will solve your rational problems

which is not everything but also not nothing.

Avoid sloppy thinking with the same reverence that you would avoid poverty.
