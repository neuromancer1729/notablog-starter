Ingredients

VITAMIN-A (Palmitate)

Vitamin A contributes to the health of your skin and scalp by promoting blood oxygen circulation.

It is one of the essential vitamins required by our bodies. It has powerful antioxidant properties. It increases oxygen circulation inside the cells helping them to stay healthy. Healthy cells are required by our bodies to generate healthier, beautiful and stronger hair, nails and skin and to handle daily wear and tear.

VITAMIN-C (Ascorbic Acid)

Vitamin C helps build collagen, which is highly important for hair growth and maintains the strength of the hair.

It is also a powerful antioxidant and fights free radicals that are responsible for cell damages and early aging. Vitamin C helps in preventing cold, chronic diseases and several types of cancers. It plays a crucial role in the formation of collagen which is required for healthy hair, skin, and nails.

Vitamin-D

Plays a crucial role in maintaining calcium and phosphorus levels in the blood, vitamin D helps in the formation of active compound calcitriol, which plays an important role in maintaining healthy skin, hair, and nails.

Vitamin-E

Vitamin E is essential for blood circulation and cell health. It is often known as a beauty vitamin because it helps in reducing wrinkles and fine lines on your skin. Like vitamin a and c, it has powerful antioxidant properties which help in the protection of cells against the free radicals.

VITAMIN-B5 (Pantothenic Acid )

Vitamin B5 helps to provide moisture to our hair keeping it healthy and smooth.

Vitamin B-5 is an essential hair, skin & nail vitamin as it helps in maintaining the natural keratin in our bodies. Natural keratin is essential for preserving the original color and texture of our skin, nails, and hair. It also helps in nourishing the cells keeping them healthy. If you want healthy, shiny, and lustrous hair.

VITAMIN-B6

Vitamin B6 is a real beauty boon for the hair and skin. It makes them soft, smooth and helps in retaining moisture for a longer duration. It also helps in blocking DHT, a substance responsible for early hair loss and the inability to form new hair follicles. Vitamin B6 can be found in chicken, fish, eggs, oatmeal, etc.

Vitamin B-9 (FOLIC ACID)

Folic Acid is a wonderful vitamin that is required by our bodies for repairing DNA. Folic acid helps in reducing hair loss and promotes the thickening of hair by actively promoting cell divisions. Folic acid works best when combined with Vitamin B12. Folic acid also promotes the formation of Red blood cells and is crucial for the development of the fetus in the womb. Folic acid is commonly found in eggs, banana, spinach, beans, etc.

BIOTIN (Vitamin H)

Biotin is a B vitamin often recommended for hair health. Since biotin deficiency can lead to thinning of the hair,

Plays an important role in hair, skin and nail health. Researchers have found that adding biotin to your daily diet can help in maintaining healthier skin, hair, and nails. Biotin is found in minute quantity in several food sources such as almonds, bananas, eggs, milk. Etc.

VITAMIN-B12

Vitamin B12 helps in the formation of red blood cells which in turn boost hair growth.

At the base of hair follicles, we have tiny blood vessels that connect to the roots of each hair strand. These blood vessels carry oxygen to the hair, thereby boosting hair growth and preventing hair fall. It also helps with memory, anxiety, and weakness.
